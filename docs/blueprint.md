# **App Name**: Mafhh Legal Connect

## Core Features:

- Service Category & Details: Comprehensive presentation of paralegal services (Small Claims, Landlord/Tenant, Traffic) with detailed descriptions, ensuring clear understanding for potential clients.
- Secure Client Inquiry Form: A contact form for general inquiries, allowing clients to securely submit their contact information and legal questions directly to Mafhh Legal. This information is stored for follow-up.
- Online Consultation Booking: Streamlined booking system for consultations, enabling clients to select preferred service types, dates, and times for appointments, with data stored securely.
- Informational Content Pages: Dedicated sections for 'About Us' (Mission, Vision, Values, Founder's Bio) and 'Client Testimonials' to build trust and inform visitors about Mafhh Legal's ethos and client satisfaction.
- AI Legal Assistant Tool: An AI-powered tool providing instant answers to frequently asked questions about common legal processes and paralegal services, guiding users to relevant information efficiently.
- Admin Dashboard for Leads: A basic internal dashboard for Mafhh Legal staff to view and manage contact form submissions and appointment requests received through the website.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color Anchor: Trust, Professionalism, Value. The theme utilizes a dark color scheme.
- Primary Color (interactive elements, accents): Rich Gold, Hex: #D7A73F (HSL: 45, 80%, 52%).
- Background Color: Deep Navy Blue, Hex: #0F1426 (HSL: 218, 55%, 10%).
- Accent Color (for highlights and secondary interactions): Warm Orange, Hex: #E67742 (HSL: 15, 70%, 55%).
- Headline Font: 'Playfair Display' (serif) for an elegant, high-end feel. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Body Font: 'Source Sans 3' (sans-serif) for clear readability and a modern aesthetic. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Utilize minimalist, line-based icons from 'Lucide React' (or similar) to maintain a modern and clean flat design aesthetic.
- Flat design approach emphasizing clean lines, generous white space, and a clear, hierarchical content presentation. Implement responsive layouts for seamless experience across all devices.
- Subtle, smooth transitions and hover effects for interactive elements to enhance user engagement without being distracting. Implement understated scroll animations for content sections.
